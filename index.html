About the Project

This website provides information about disclosing the use of generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) in digital humanities research. It includes guidelines, best practices, and resources for researchers and institutions to ensure transparency and ethical use of GenAI technologies.

Data Collection

The data used in this project was collected from various sources, including academic publications, surveys, and interviews with digital humanities researchers. The data is analyzed to understand current practices and attitudes towards GenAI use and disclosure.
